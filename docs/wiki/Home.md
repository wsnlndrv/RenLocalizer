## ✨ Features at a Glance

*   🔹 **Multi-Engine Support:** Google, DeepL, OpenAI, Gemini, and Local LLMs.
*   🔹 **Smart Parsing:** Protects `[variables]` and `{tags}` automatically.
*   🔹 **Deep Scanning:** Finds hidden strings in `init python` blocks.
*   🔹 **Translation Memory:** Reuse translations across games — import from any `tl/` folder.
*   🔹 **Cross-Platform:** Runs natively on all major operating systems.
*   🔹 **Open Source:** Licensed under GPL-3.0.
